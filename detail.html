Super-Normal

Super-normal is an utterly ordinary house that sticks to essentials instead of fancy design elements. It was designed as a minimal house with basic functions based on each family member’s lifestyle under circumstances where the site was not selected.

The building owner bought a vacant lot in Yangpyeong in 2018. The local contractor involved, however, failed to take steps in a timely manner, so the owner had to wait long to acquire a development permit and get civil engineering work to begin. The owner, who could not delay his moving plan any further, sold the lot and started to look for alternative lots nearby. This is why we needed to design a house by taking uncertainties into account; we set a few basic rules to define the exterior space and elevation and then planned a design focused on essentials, such as terrace, lighting, interactive interior, and yard.

The building is very simple in form. The fact that there were no restrictions on slant lines for daylight and the average size and placement of lots and density of general houses in housing sites near Sinhwa-ri, Yangpyeong, provided us with much leeway in deciding on the appearance and materials of the house. A terrace was designed to make connections between every interior space to the exterior space to help the family appreciate the nature, in the shape of a pixelized hat that becomes narrower toward the top. a large square window is created on the elevation facing south to eliminate a sense of hierarchy of scales by maximizing the view and lighting. The stand-alone wooden cylinder is used as a home studio.

Super-Normal, planned to be an ordinary, common object, has a structure that prepares it for an undefined site. The centrality of the common space and the peripherality of the private rooms and terrace determine the composition, and structural load bearing and non-load-bearing walls are coordinated accordingly. The area of the programs and the size of the terrace are variable so that the physical form of the building can be adjusted to the site. There was no particular siting issue when the site was selected. With sites nearby being almost even in size and being placed based on directions and with its variability, Super-Normal turns into a product that is ‘Beyond Ordinary.’
